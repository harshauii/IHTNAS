How to Publish a Research Paper in IHTNAS?

Submit Paper

Review Process

Paper review by the reviewer committee.

Acceptance Status

If a paper is accepted, the author will be notified via their registered email address.

Processing Charges

Pay paper processing charges as per mentioned in the acceptance letter.

Copyright Submission

Submit the signed copyright and undertaking forms.

Published

The paper will be published, and the author will be notified via their registered email address.
